# CPKit Design System Guidelines

This document outlines the styling parameters, theme tokens, and typography guidelines for CPKit.

---

## 🎨 Theme Colors (HSL Tokens)

CPKit relies on a Tailwind CSS v4 variables configuration.

### Dark Mode (Core Palette)
- **Background**: `hsl(240 10% 3.9%)` (Rich Onyx dark canvas)
- **Card / Popovers**: `hsl(240 10% 3.9% / 65%)` (Translucent glassmorphism)
- **Primary Accent**: `hsl(217.2 91.2% 59.8%)` (Vibrant Blue highlights)
- **Success Tone**: `hsl(142.1 76.2% 36.3%)` (Emerald Green)

---

## 📐 Spacing & Borders

- **Padding standard**: `p-4` or `p-6` for component card bodies.
- **Radius standard**: `rounded-2xl` (`16px`) for visualizer cards, `rounded-xl` for inner selectors.
- **Divider standard**: `border-border/10` or `border-border/40` for crisp dark dividers.
