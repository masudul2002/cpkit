# CPKit Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the official guidelines for the visual brand identity of CPKit.

---

## 🎨 Color Specifications (HEX & HSL)

- **Bracket Left Blue**: `#1D4ED8` | `hsl(221.2 83.2% 53.3%)`
- **Bracket Right Cyan**: `#06B6D4` | `hsl(188.7 86.1% 42.4%)`
- **Purple Nodes**: `#8B5CF6` | `hsl(258.5 90.3% 66%)`

---

## 📐 Sizing & Spacing

- **Minimum Navbar Sizing**: The logo height should not drop below `24px`.
- **Favicon dimensions**: Rendered pixel-perfect at `16px` and `32px` containers.
- **Clearance Spacing**: Keep at least `12px` of empty clear margins surrounding the monogram icon.

---

## 🚫 Brand Taboos (Don'ts)

- **No Stretching**: Do not scale the vector proportions asymmetrically.
- **No Complex Shadows**: Avoid placing drop-shadows or 3D glows around the clean vector node lines.
